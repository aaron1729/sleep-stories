Established back in the year thirteen eighty-three during King Keu Naone's rule, the temple, much like Doi Suthep itself, has quietly observed the vicinity's evolution. They commence on a tranquil, contemplative stroll around the golden chedi, radiating a gentle aura of tranquility that grounds us further in our awe-inspiring environment.
